Season Overview

The first season of My Hero Academia introduces us to the world where 80% of the population has superpowers called "Quirks." We follow Izuku Midoriya, a quirkless boy who dreams of becoming a hero, as he inherits the legendary One For All power from his idol All Might and begins his journey at U.A. High School.
