Background

Born without a Quirk in a world where 80% of people have superpowers, Izuku Midoriya never gave up on his dream of becoming a hero. Despite being constantly bullied and told he could never be a hero, his determination and analytical mind set him apart.

Everything changed when he encountered a villain attacking his childhood friend Katsuki Bakugo. Without thinking of his own safety, Izuku rushed in to help, displaying the true spirit of a hero. This selfless act caught the attention of All Might, the world's greatest hero, who saw in Izuku the qualities needed to inherit the legendary Quirk "One For All."
